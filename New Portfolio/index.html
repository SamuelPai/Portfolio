About Me

Hi my name is Sam Pai! I was born and raised in sunny San Diego California where I attended Torrey Pines High School.

After graduating, I decided to attend UC Davis in northern California. It was there that I received my bachelors of arts in Communications. After graduating college, I pursued a career in digital marketing and worked as both a social media marketing specialist as well as an SEO specialist.

In my spare time I play basketball and I am also a part time online fitness coach!
